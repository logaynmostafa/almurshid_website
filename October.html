VISION & MISSION

VISION

October 6 University seeks to be a pioneer edifice for distinctive high education in domestic and regional level.

MISSION

October 6 University is committed to provide graduates with the necessary efficiency and skills that cater for the requirements of job market To achieve its mission. the University adopts and apples educational programmes with reference standards that would provide students with distinctive knowledge and skills which in return would enhance their competitive ability. The University also provides students with a distinctive educational environment that support their continual learning and training processes. October 6 University is also committed to serve community and develop environment in different forms of community engagement and applicable research work through forging collaborative partnerships at both domestic and international levels
